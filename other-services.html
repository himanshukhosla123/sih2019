Whatsapp for Services

This is an initiative designed to empower customers to access their policies and provide them a host of services over WhatsApp ranging from policy sharing, Aadhar/PAN document verification to policy renewal, premium alerts, document collection and receiving important updates.

REVA (Robotics Enabled Virtual Assistant)

With the help of natural language conversion feature, REVA will function like an insurance guide to assist customers through their policy buying and policy enquiry process, providing solutions to queries in real time.

Online Insurance Procedure

Now you can manage your insurance policies with just a click within the comfort of your home!
